Popular Pieces

Feminist Frequency

A non-profit organization run by Anita Sarkeesian, they create a web video series that explores and discusses women's roles in pop culture, particularly video games. Feminist Frequency became well known when a backlash erupted over their beginning, threatening the lives of those involved in the creation of this organization.
